# Subscription Tiers

## "Lumia Lite"
**Your first step into organized memories**

### Storage
- 2GB total storage

### Media Quality
- Photos: Compressed to 1080p resolution (maintains original aspect ratio)
- Videos: Compressed to 720p resolution, maximum 30fps

## "Lumia Glow"
**Enhanced clarity for your growing collection**

### Storage
- 20GB total storage

### Media Quality
- Photos: Original resolution up to 12MP, larger photos optimized
- Videos: Compressed to 1080p resolution, original framerate preserved

## "Lumia Aurora"
**Brilliant, uncompromised family memories**

### Storage
- 100GB total storage

### Media Quality
- Photos: Original resolution preserved, larger photos optimized
- Videos: Original resolution preserved, original framerate preserved
